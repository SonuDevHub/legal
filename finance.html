Finance & Real Estate Legal Advisory

Gavel N Grace’s Finance and Real Estate team assists clients in navigating the intricate legal frameworks governing property ownership, investment, and financial structuring. Our partners provide precise legal solutions that protect your assets, minimize risks, and ensure regulatory compliance in every transaction.

Our finance and real estate advisory practice combines deep industry knowledge with legal acumen to guide clients through complex transactions. From due diligence and documentation to title verification and registration, we ensure transparency and security at every step. We act as trusted advisors for investors, developers, lenders, and private clients.

Our key areas of expertise include:

  • Real estate acquisition, sale, and leasing
  • Title verification and property due diligence
  • Project financing and mortgage structuring
  • Drafting and negotiation of loan and security documents
  • Regulatory compliance under RERA and land laws
  • Banking transactions and debt recovery

We represent clients before authorities such as RERA, DRT, and revenue departments, ensuring legal clarity in matters related to property ownership and financial disputes. Our legal team also handles real estate litigation, recovery suits, and compliance audits.

At Gavel N Grace, we combine precision with practicality — ensuring your financial and real estate dealings are structured, compliant, and future-secure. Our approach is client-focused, risk-aware, and driven by long-term value creation.
