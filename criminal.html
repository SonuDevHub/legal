Criminal Defense & White-Collar Litigation

Gavel N Grace’s Criminal Defense team provides strong, discreet, and strategic representation for individuals and corporations facing criminal allegations. Our seasoned partners bring decades of courtroom experience and a deep understanding of procedural law to protect our clients’ rights and reputations in high-stakes cases.

We believe that every case deserves an unwavering defense and fair representation. Our team handles matters ranging from individual offenses to complex white-collar and corporate criminal investigations. We maintain absolute confidentiality and strategic precision in every stage — from investigation and pre-trial to appeals.

Our practice covers a wide range of criminal matters, including:

  • Economic offenses and fraud investigations
  • Cybercrime, data theft, and digital evidence cases
  • Cheating, forgery, and criminal breach of trust
  • Defamation and reputation-related litigation
  • Money laundering and financial irregularities
  • Corporate criminal liability and director defense

Gavel N Grace’s defense partners are known for their strategic approach and relentless pursuit of justice. We work closely with forensic experts, compliance officers, and investigators to build strong cases supported by evidence and legal insight. Whether it’s securing bail, quashing an FIR, or leading a complex defense, our team is prepared to act swiftly and effectively.

We value integrity, preparedness, and precision — ensuring our clients’ legal battles are handled with the utmost professionalism and respect for due process.
